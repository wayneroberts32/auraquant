Connecting Brokers

Supported Brokers:

  • Alpaca: US Stocks & Crypto - API Key + Secret required
  • Binance: Cryptocurrency - API Key + Secret required
  • Interactive Brokers: Global Markets - TWS API setup
  • NAB Bank: Australian Markets - OAuth connection

Risk Management

Understanding the 2% Drawdown Rule

Core Rule: Maximum 2% Portfolio Drawdown

The bot will NEVER allow your account to lose more than 2% from its peak value.

How It Works:

  • Per Trade: Maximum 1.25% risk per position
  • Portfolio Level: Maximum 2% total drawdown
  • Automatic Protection: Bot closes positions if approaching limit
  • Emergency Stop: All trading halts at 1.8% drawdown

Example:

Account Value: $10,000

Max Loss per Trade: $125 (1.25%)

Max Total Loss: $200 (2%)

Emergency Stop at: $180 loss (1.8%)

Global Compliance Rules

Automatic Compliance Enforcement:

  • US PDT Rule: $25k minimum for day trading
  • Australian ASIC: Leverage limits on CFDs
  • EU MiFID II: Risk warnings and limits
  • Crypto Regulations: KYC/AML compliance

Bot automatically detects your jurisdiction and applies strictest applicable rules.

Backup & Restore
